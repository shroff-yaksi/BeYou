Warrior II Pose

A powerful standing pose that strengthens the legs and core while improving focus and stamina. It builds balance and stability.

Steps

1

Stand with feet wide apart, about 4 feet. Turn your right foot out 90 degrees.

2

Inhale and lift your arms out to the sides, parallel to the floor, palms down.

3

Exhale and bend your right knee over the right ankle, keeping the leg vertical.

4

Turn your head to the right and look out over your fingers.
